About Us:

In today's rapidly growing and truly global software industry, success is now more than ever dependent on the quality of the software. A good quality product or service leads to satisfied and loyal customers, the main ambition of every company. The challenge that most IT managers and executives have is common: how to effectively test software applications on time and within budget while balancing resources? Under the right circumstances, crowdsourcing offers a rapid and cost-effective approach to software testing. Traditionally, software testing was a neglected afterthought. It was a tedious chore for developers once their real work was done and was based on a set of formal verification mechanisms that were costly to apply. Several trends are changing the perception in favor or crowd sourced testing. The advent of web applications, whereby software is deployed on the Internet and bugs become apparent very quickly. We provide a crowd-based mobile testing community to support mobile testing Services.
